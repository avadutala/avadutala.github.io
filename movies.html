Movies

Movies that stayed with me.

Some films stay because of story. Some because of music. Some because they open a window into history, memory, love, fear, or imagination.

The Shawshank Redemption

A timeless story about hope, patience, and quiet resilience. It is one of those films that stays relevant no matter when I watch it.

Interstellar

More than science fiction, this film is a deeply emotional story about time, space, and the bond between a father and daughter.

Eternal Sunshine of the Spotless Mind

A unique exploration of memory, love, and loss. It makes me think about whether forgetting pain is ever truly worth it.

Sakhi

My all-time favorite. A film that beautifully captures love, individuality, and emotional strength in a way that feels personal every time.

Memento

A brilliant narrative experiment that challenges how we understand memory, truth, and reality.

The Pianist

A powerful and haunting portrayal of survival during one of the darkest periods in history.

Vertigo

Classic Hitchcock: psychological, layered, and visually unforgettable.

Mad Max: Fury Road

Pure cinematic energy. Minimal dialogue, maximum impact, and a world that feels completely alive.

Atonement

A story of love, misunderstanding, and consequences. Emotionally heavy, beautifully told, and difficult to forget.

The English Patient

A slow, deeply emotional film about love, memory, and loss across time.

The Others

A haunting and atmospheric film that builds tension through silence, mood, and storytelling.

Amores Perros

Raw and intense storytelling that connects lives in unexpected and powerful ways.

Prometheus

Ambitious and thought-provoking science fiction. It raises more questions than answers, which is part of why it stays interesting.

Big Fish

A beautiful blend of imagination and reality, about storytelling, life, and relationships.

The Good, the Bad and the Ugly

A classic that defines its genre. Style, music, and storytelling working together at their best.

Psycho

One of the most influential thrillers ever made. Simple, sharp, unsettling, and unforgettable.

Rear Window

A masterclass in suspense built through observation, curiosity, and a single setting.

Dil Se

Emotionally intense, with music and storytelling that stay close to the heart.

Nayakudu

A powerful portrayal of leadership, morality, and human complexity. Mani Ratnam at an unforgettable level.

Nireekshana

Elevated by Ilaiyaraaja’s music, this film creates a deeply emotional and atmospheric experience.

Life Is Beautiful

A rare combination of humor and tragedy, showing love and sacrifice in the darkest situations.

A Beautiful Mind

An inspiring story about brilliance, struggle, love, and perseverance.

What I want to watch

I am always drawn to science fiction. Any movie that explores time, space, memory, artificial intelligence, evolution, or the future of humanity immediately gets my attention.
