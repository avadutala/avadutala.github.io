Places I have lived in or visited

Vizag, India

My hometown — born and raised there, with childhood memories, family, and Bubblu, my pet dog.

Hyderabad, India

The home of biryani, and naturally one of my favorite food memories.

Bangalore, India

My first job, unlimited buffets, and my introduction to Black Forest ice cream.

Chennai, India

Temples, beaches, Anjappar, food, and the first days of marriage.

Pune, India

The last city before moving abroad — great food, movies, and transition memories.

Karlskrona, Sweden

My first international city during my master’s studies, and my first glimpse of snow.

Gothenburg, Sweden

A city I visited, though it did not connect with me as strongly as others.

Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

Petronas Towers, local restaurants, and a full year of discovering Malaysian life.

Petaling Jaya, Malaysia

A place where I worked and lived for more than a year.

Langkawi, Malaysia

A beautiful island escape where I spent a few peaceful days.

Bangkok, Thailand

Home for five years, where my second daughter was born and Thai food became a lifelong favorite.

Phuket, Thailand

World-class beaches and weeks of vacation memories.

Toronto, Canada

My first North American city, and the beginning of a new chapter without a job.

London, Ontario

My first international job and an important step in Canada.

Windsor, Canada

My first house in Canada, great friends, backyard life, snow shoveling, lawn mowing, and my first Tesla.

Detroit, USA

Worked at Chrysler while living in Canada — my first real glimpse of the United States.

Phoenix, USA

Big cactus, heat, Nikola Motors, year-round tennis, food everywhere, and a city that felt like heaven.

Southwest USA

Las Vegas, Los Angeles, San Diego, Grand Canyon, and Flagstaff — including my first time skiing.

Rocky Point, Mexico

Beach time, border drives, and relaxed memories by the water.

Hawaii

Simply wow — Jurassic Park forests, clean beaches, great food, and fresh passion fruit.

Hanoi, Vietnam

Culture, food, busy streets, old neighborhoods, and a completely different rhythm of life.

Hong Kong

Skyline, energy, speed, cultural depth, and unforgettable food.

Places I want to visit

Japan

For sushi, culture, and a trip my daughters would love.

Australia

A continent-country with unique animals found nowhere else.

Europe by road

A long-time dream: drive across many countries and experience them slowly.

China

Food, culture, and one of the great cuisines of the world.

Middle East

Food again — flavors, history, and culture.

Brazil

The Amazon forest, because I spent my childhood near forests and want to see the world’s largest one.
