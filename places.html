Travel & memory

Places visited, lived in, and learned from.

Every place has a memory: food, weather, family, work, friends, beaches, snow, tennis, or the first time seeing something completely new.

Places I have lived in or visited

Vizag, India

Home town

Born and raised here. Home, childhood, and memories with my pet dog Bubblu.

Hyderabad, India

Biryani city

One of my favorite food memories. Hyderabad is home to biryani, and biryani is one of my favorites.

Bangalore, India

First job

My first job, unlimited buffet memories, and an introduction to Black Forest ice creams.

Chennai, India

Marriage, temples, beaches, food

Temples, Anjappar, beaches, food, and the first days of marriage.

Pune, India

Last Indian city before moving abroad

Great food, movies, and the last city I lived in before moving abroad.

Karlskrona, Sweden

First international home

The first country and city I stayed in outside India during my master’s education. Also my first glimpse of snow.

Gothenburg, Sweden

Visited

A city I visited during my Sweden chapter. Not every place becomes a favorite, and that is part of travel too.

Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia

Petronas Towers and local food

First time seeing the Petronas Towers. I spent a year eating at many local restaurants nearby.

Petaling Jaya, Malaysia

Work and daily life

Worked and stayed there for more than a year.

Langkawi, Malaysia

Beautiful island

Visited and stayed for a few days. A beautiful place.

Bangkok, Thailand

Family, food, and entrepreneurship

My second daughter was born here. I stayed for five years, my first daughter learned and spoke Thai, and this is where I fell in love with Thai food.

Phuket, Thailand

World-class beaches

Spent weeks vacationing there. World-class beaches and unforgettable holidays.

Toronto, Canada

First North American city

The first North American city I lived in — and I arrived there without a job.

London, Ontario

First international job

The city where I got my first job internationally.

Windsor, Canada

First house in Canada

My first house in Canada, and the first place I truly liked after my hometown. Great friends, a huge backyard, snow shoveling, lawn mowing, my first Tesla, and the start of a great life.

Detroit, USA

Work across the border

Worked at Chrysler in Detroit while staying in Canada. My first glimpses of the USA.

Phoenix, USA

Desert life and tennis

Drove from Canada to Phoenix and saw giant cactus for the first time. I did not know cactus could be that big. Started working with Nikola, discovered the heat, and found a place where I can play tennis year-round. Food everywhere, Mexico nearby — it feels like heaven.

Las Vegas, Los Angeles, San Diego, Grand Canyon, Flagstaff

Southwest memories

Many road trips, repeated visits to the Grand Canyon, and my first time skiing in Flagstaff.

Rocky Point / Puerto Peñasco, Mexico

Beach time

Spent good time at the beach near the Mexico border.

Hawaii

Simply wow

Jurassic Park forests, beautiful food, clean beaches, and passion fruits. Simply wow.
